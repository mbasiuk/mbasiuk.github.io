BAS-A - Independent private party demand confirmation tool.

The purpose of this tool is to provide independent third-party confirmation for party demand such as payment confirmations, contract terminations, demand from insurance companies and other on-demand activities. Using this tool reduces the likelihood of either party missing or denying the existence of the demand actions.

Form BAS-A Description
1 Read No. Identifier for the specific BAS-A readonly form. Parties may share it for reference in agreements, negotiations, and disputes. Generated by BAS-A tool. Unique, read only.
2 Edit No. Identifier for specific BAS-A editable form. Party having the identifier can call demand action. Generated by BAS-A tool
3 Created Date and time when the form was created, using the current viewer's timezone. Example: 5/24/2024, 10:26:41 PM
3 Is Demanded Shows "no" when action was not demanded, and "yes" when it was already demanded. Once a demand action is called, it is irreversible.
5 Demanded Show date when demand action was triggered. Example: "-". Example: 5/24/2024, 10:26:41 PM
6 Expire Example: 5/24/2024, 10:26:41 PM. Once expired, the form is unaligible to display or load, and don't descrease probability of other party ignoring or denying existence of the demand action.
7 Loaded Date and Time when the form was loaded. Example: 5/24/2024, 10:26:41 PM
8 Timezone Current viewer's timezone. Used to display field 3, 5, 6 and 7.
Q: How to use this tool?
A: Tool owner is expecting that there will be 2 patries (Party A and Party B) who will use reference for the form BAS-A using field 1 No in their agreement, negotiations and disputes, and party B should take aproptiate action agreed by parties, once party A will demand for it. Party A will receive confirmation for demand by updating form BAS-A using field 2 Edit No, and making a references to the date of this action using form BAS-A field 5 - demanded. Form field 4 Expire is indicating a date when it's expired, and this record will not be served by the tool. The tool may be utilized during legal disputes, including court cases, and the parties agree not to assert its use as a violation of privacy. Any such assertion shall be deemed unenforceable.
Q: who should create the form?
A: party who might to call for on-demand action creating BAS-A form.
Q: Who can demand an action?
A: field 3. Edit No. used to demand an action
Q: What will happened if BAS-A will fail to work? Cases like data loss, going out of bussiness, or BAS-A will sold to other party.
A: The agreement which are refering to BAS-A shold be written in a way, that missing this confirmation will not harm either party interests. Avoid BAS-A agreement to be required or only options for handle demand action. but as confirmation from BAS-A independent party for this action to be called.
